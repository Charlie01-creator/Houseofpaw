Frequently Asked Questions

How do I adopt a dog from HOUSE OF PAWS?

Our adoption process begins with completing an application, followed by a meet-and-greet, home visit, and adoption contract. Visit our adoption center or check our website for available dogs. The entire process typically takes 3-7 days.

What should I do if I find a stray dog?

If the dog appears friendly, check for ID tags. You can contact local shelters or our emergency line. If the dog seems injured or aggressive, contact animal control for assistance. Never approach an unknown dog if you feel unsafe.

Do you accept owner surrenders?

Yes, by appointment only. We ask owners to complete a surrender form and provide any medical records. There may be a surrender fee to help cover care costs. We encourage owners to explore all alternatives before surrendering.

Can I specify how my donation is used?

Absolutely! You can designate your gift for medical care, facility upkeep, or a specific program. Unrestricted donations allow us to allocate funds where most needed. All donations are tax-deductible.

Animal Emergency?

For after-hours emergencies involving injured or abused animals, please call our emergency line: (555) 911-PAWS
